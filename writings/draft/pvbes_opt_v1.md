### Literature Review: Optimization of Photovoltaic-Battery Energy Storage Systems

#### Literature 1 [Achaibou et al., 2023]
**Research Focus**: Accurate parameter identification of lead-acid battery models for PV applications.
**Methodology**: Enhanced Marine Predator Algorithm (MPA) for parameter optimization.
**Key Findings**: MPA achieved 99.99% identification efficiency, outperforming SSA, AEO, and HGS algorithms.
**Citation**: Achaibou N, Ferahtia S, Djerioui A, Chouder A, Rezk H, Houari A. Optimal parameters identification strategy of a lead acid battery model for photovoltaic applications. Energy Storage 2023;5:e428. https://doi.org/10.1002/est2.428

#### Literature 2 [Ahmed et al., 2024]
**Research Focus**: Classification and parametric optimization of hybrid PV-thermal (PVT) systems.
**Methodology**: Novel multi-dimensional classification model integrating thermodynamic and electrochemical parameters.
**Key Findings**: System efficiency improved by 15-20%, with thermal efficiency reaching 45% and PV efficiency 18%.
**Citation**: Ahmed M, Rashel M, Islam M, Islam A, Tlemcani M. Classification and Parametric Analysis of Solar Hybrid PVT System: A Review. Energies 2024;17:588. https://doi.org/10.3390/en17030588

#### Literature 3 [Alramlawi and Li, 2020]
**Research Focus**: Lead-acid battery lifetime prediction in residential microgrids.
**Methodology**: Electrochemistry-based weighted Ah throughput model.
**Key Findings**: Battery lifetime prediction error reduced from ±30% to ±8% compared to traditional models.
**Citation**: Alramlawi M, Li P. Design Optimization of a Residential PV-Battery Microgrid With a Detailed Battery Lifetime Estimation Model. IEEE Trans on Ind Applicat 2020;56:2020–30. https://doi.org/10.1109/TIA.2020.2965894

#### Literature 4 [Argyrou et al., 2021]
**Research Focus**: Dynamic stability of PV-supercapacitor hybrid storage systems.
**Methodology**: Small-signal model-based current controller design (Boost/Buck dual-mode).
**Key Findings**: DC bus voltage ripple <±0.625%, response time <0.2 seconds.
**Citation**: Argyrou MC, Marouchos CC, Kalogirou SA, Christodoulides P. Modeling a residential grid-connected PV system with battery–supercapacitor storage: Control design and stability analysis. Energy Reports 2021;7:4988–5002. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.egyr.2021.08.001

#### Literature 5 [Bai et al., 2019]
**Research Focus**: Economic feasibility of repurposed EV batteries in distributed PV.
**Methodology**: Multi-scenario NPV model considering self-consumption and peak-shaving strategies.
**Key Findings**: NPV increased by 23-27% in commercial scenarios with peak-valley price difference >0.15$/kWh.
**Citation**: Bai B, Xiong S, Song B, Xiaoming M. Economic analysis of distributed solar photovoltaics with reused electric vehicle batteries as energy storage systems in China. Renewable and Sustainable Energy Reviews 2019;109:213–29. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.rser.2019.03.048

#### Literature 6 [Chen et al., 2024]
**Research Focus**: Capacity optimization of hybrid cooling-battery storage for commercial buildings.
**Methodology**: Two-layer optimization model prioritizing cooling storage with battery supplementation.
**Key Findings**: Optimal cooling storage capacity ratio 40%, total system cost reduced by 56.3%.
**Citation**: Chen Q, Kuang Z, Liu X, Zhang T. Optimal sizing and techno-economic analysis of the hybrid PV-battery-cooling storage system for commercial buildings in China. Applied Energy 2024;355:122231. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.apenergy.2023.122231

#### Literature 7 [Cui et al., 2023]
**Research Focus**: Efficiency enhancement of solar thermal-CAES systems.
**Methodology**: Three-stage waste heat recovery strategy combining ORC and heat pump optimization.
**Key Findings**: System efficiency improved from baseline 32% to 38.9%.
**Citation**: Cui F, An D, Teng S, Lin X, Li D, Xi H. Cogeneration systems of solar energy integrated with compressed air energy storage systems: A comparative study of various energy recovery strategies. Case Studies in Thermal Engineering 2023;51:103521. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.csite.2023.103521

#### Literature 8 [Diab et al., 2023]
**Research Focus**: PV utilization optimization in trolleybus power networks.
**Methodology**: Energy-transit KPI-based rapid assessment model using cubic polynomial fitting.
**Key Findings**: Maximum direct PV utilization rate 38%, limited by vehicle scheduling sequence.
**Citation**: Diab I, Saffirio A, Chandra-Mouli GR, Bauer P. A simple method for sizing and estimating the performance of PV systems in trolleybus grids. Journal of Cleaner Production 2023;384:135623. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jclepro.2022.135623

#### Literature 9 [Dufo-López et al., 2021]
**Research Focus**: Comparative validation of lead-acid and Li-ion battery lifetime models.
**Methodology**: Weighted Ah throughput model (lead-acid) vs. cycle+calendar aging model (Li-ion).
**Key Findings**: Li-ion lifetime double that of lead-acid (13 years vs. 5 years in Tindouf region).
**Citation**: Dufo-López R, Cortés-Arcos T, Artal-Sevil JS, Bernal-Agustín JL. Comparison of Lead-Acid and Li-Ion Batteries Lifetime Prediction Models in Stand-Alone Photovoltaic Systems. Applied Sciences 2021;11:1099. https://doi.org/10.3390/app11031099

#### Literature 10 [El Mezdi et al., 2023]
**Research Focus**: MPPT and battery charge-discharge coordination control.
**Methodology**: Backstepping controller with ANN-MPPT algorithm.
**Key Findings**: SOC tracking error <1%, grid power factor >0.99.
**Citation**: El Mezdi K, El Magri A, Watil A, El Myasse I, Bahatti L, Lajouad R, et al. Nonlinear control design and stability analysis of hybrid grid-connected photovoltaic-Battery energy storage system with ANN-MPPT method. Journal of Energy Storage 2023;72:108747. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.est.2023.108747

#### Literature 11 [El Sayed et al., 2024]
**Research Focus**: Distributed energy resource optimization in radial networks.
**Methodology**: Improved Particle Swarm Optimization (IPSO) two-layer framework.
**Key Findings**: Total cost reduced by 56.3%, network losses decreased by 68.16%.
**Citation**: El Sayed A, Ahmed EEE, Poyrazoglu G. Optimal Planning and Allocation of DER in Radial Distribution Networks Using IPSO. 2024 IEEE 8th Energy Conference (ENERGYCON), Doha, Qatar: IEEE; 2024, p. 1–6. https://doi.org/10.1109/ENERGYCON58629.2024.10488786

#### Literature 12 [Furlan and You, 2024]
**Research Focus**: Economic and technical risk balancing in hybrid solar systems.
**Methodology**: Two-stage stochastic robust optimization combining probabilistic scenarios and robust constraints.
**Key Findings**: System cost volatility reduced by 40% (LCOE 0.12-0.18$/kWh at 95% confidence).
**Citation**: Furlan G, You F. Robust design of hybrid solar power systems: Sustainable integration of concentrated solar power and photovoltaic technologies. Advances in Applied Energy 2024;13:100164. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.adapen.2024.100164

#### Literature 13 [He et al., 2023]
**Research Focus**: Systematic capacity optimization for off-grid hybrid systems.
**Methodology**: Bibliometric analysis (VOSviewer) and multi-objective optimization classification.
**Key Findings**: Developed universal optimization model with 4 objective functions covering 90% of existing research.
**Citation**: He Y, Guo S, Dong P, Zhang Y, Huang J, Zhou J. A state-of-the-art review and bibliometric analysis on the sizing optimization of off-grid hybrid renewable energy systems. Renewable and Sustainable Energy Reviews 2023;183:113476. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.rser.2023.113476

#### Literature 14 [Ibrahim et al., 2024]
**Research Focus**: Multi-microgrid renewable energy and storage coordination.
**Methodology**: Enhanced NSGA-III algorithm considering 24 load patterns.
**Key Findings**: Total cost reduced by 19.3%, renewable penetration increased to 78% (Tokyo case).
**Citation**: Ibrahim NN, Jamian JJ, Md Rasid M. Optimal multi-objective sizing of renewable energy sources and battery energy storage systems for formation of a multi-microgrid system considering diverse load patterns. Energy 2024;304:131921. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.energy.2024.131921

#### Literature 15 [Khalilpour and Vassallo, 2016]
**Research Focus**: Economic sensitivity analysis of PV-battery systems.
**Methodology**: Parametric Monte Carlo simulation considering electricity price, solar radiation, and battery cost variations.
**Key Findings**: Battery cost must decrease below 200$/kWh (current 300-400$/kWh) for economic viability.
**Citation**: Khalilpour KR, Vassallo A. Technoeconomic parametric analysis of PV-battery systems. Renewable Energy 2016;97:757–68. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.renene.2016.06.010

#### Literature 16 [Liang et al., 2024]
**Research Focus**: Energy matching optimization for green data centers.
**Methodology**: PV-CAES coordinated dispatch model.
**Key Findings**: Annual carbon emissions reduced by 62.5%, overall energy efficiency increased to 81.2% (Beijing case).
**Citation**: Liang Y, Li P, Su W, Li W, Xu W. Development of green data center by configuring photovoltaic power generation and compressed air energy storage systems. Energy 2024;292:130516. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.energy.2024.130516

#### Literature 21 [Ridha et al., 2021]
**Research Focus**: Algorithm selection for off-grid PV system multi-objective optimization.
**Methodology**: Multi-criteria decision analysis (hybrid AHP-TOPSIS method).
**Key Findings**: Developed algorithm selection guide for 6 scenarios (e.g., HOMER recommended for cost-priority cases).
**Citation**: Ridha HM, Gomes C, Hizam H, Ahmadipour M, Heidari AA, Chen H. Multi-objective optimization and multi-criteria decision-making methods for optimal design of standalone photovoltaic system: A comprehensive review. Renewable and Sustainable Energy Reviews 2021;135:110202. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.rser.2020.110202

#### Literature 24 [Shabani et al., 2021]
**Research Focus**: Battery model impact on PV-storage system economic assessment.
**Methodology**: Comparison of three battery models (ideal, cycle aging, electrochemical).
**Key Findings**: System cost error reduced to ±5% with electrochemical model (vs. ±20% for traditional models).
**Citation**: Shabani M, Dahlquist E, Wallin F, Yan J. Techno-economic impacts of battery performance models and control strategies on optimal design of a grid-connected PV system. Energy Conversion and Management 2021;245:114617. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.enconman.2021.114617

#### Literature 28 [Yang et al., 2024]
**Research Focus**: Long-duration storage support quantification for PV stability.
**Methodology**: Capacity credit analysis using California PV output data.
**Key Findings**: 4-hour storage increases PV capacity credit from 40% to 75%, 12-hour storage to 90%.
**Citation**: Yang G, Yang D, Liu B, Zhang H. The role of short- and long-duration energy storage in reducing the cost of firm photovoltaic generation. Applied Energy 2024;374:123914. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.apenergy.2024.123914

#### Literature 29 [Zhao et al., 2024]
**Research Focus**: High-resolution PV-wind-storage system co-optimization.
**Methodology**: Mixed-integer programming with enhanced battery lifetime model (cycles + temperature correction).
**Key Findings**: System cost reduced by 9.8%, battery lifetime extended to 15 years in Northwest China.
**Citation**: Zhao Y-B, Dong X-J, Shen J-N, He Y-J. Simultaneous sizing and scheduling optimization for PV-wind-battery hybrid systems with a modified battery lifetime model: A high-resolution analysis in China. Applied Energy 2024;360:122812. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.apenergy.2024.122812
